Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, peeled and coarsely chopped
  • 2 brown onions, coarsely chopped
  • 3 celery sticks, coarsely chopped
  • 160g butter
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce gravy
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1/3 cup plain flour
