Long Island Iced Tea

How can you have a christmas party without some booze

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of iced cubes
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce white rum
  • 1 ounce white tequila
  • 1/2 ounce triple sec
  • 1 lemons
  • cole to taste

Steps

  1. Get cocktail shaker and pour ice and all alcohol in plus lemon juice
  2. Shake it til you make it
  3. Pour mixture into glass and add coke to taste
